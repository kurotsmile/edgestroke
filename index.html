About EdgeStroke

EdgeStroke is a lightweight web tool for creating edge-based outlines around subjects. It is useful for sticker effects, cutout designs, and fast preview workflows before final editing.

Quick Guide

  1. Choose an image from your device.
  2. Pick a stroke color and adjust threshold.
  3. Optionally choose a Quick Style preset.
  4. Click Detect Edge Stroke and download PNG.
